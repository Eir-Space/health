**Status:** Active rehabilitation needed
**Priority:** High - daily exercises required

**Daily Support Needed:**
- **Exercise reminders:** 3-4 times per day
- **Progress tracking:** Pain levels, range of motion
- **Activity modifications:** Ergonomic awareness
- **Heat/ice therapy guidance:** Timing and application

**Rehabilitation Protocol:** [`body/arms/rehabilitation/finger-rehab.md`](./body/arms/rehabilitation/finger-rehab.md)

**Key Exercises (Phase 1):**
1. **Gentle Range of Motion** - 3-4x daily, 5-10 reps
2. **Adjacent Finger Movement** - 3x daily
3. **Finger Spreading** - 3-4x daily, hold 3-5 seconds

**Warning Signs to Monitor:**
- Sharp, severe pain
- Sudden swelling or redness  
- Numbness or tingling
- No improvement after 1 week
